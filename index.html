Bell Dating App Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Bell, the dating app designed to connect people in a meaningful way. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services. This includes:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, date of birth, etc.
  • Profile Information: Photos, preferences, and other details you choose to share.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app.
  • Location Data: If you enable location services, we collect your location for matching purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving our services.
  • Personalizing your experience and matches.
  • Communicating with you about the app and its features.
  • Ensuring the security and integrity of our services.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with third parties for specific purposes, such as:

  • Service Providers: Partners and vendors who assist us in delivering our services.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Your Choices

You can manage your privacy settings within the app, including opting out of certain data collection. Keep in mind that some features may be limited if certain permissions are not granted.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated through the app or our website.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@belldatingapp.com.
